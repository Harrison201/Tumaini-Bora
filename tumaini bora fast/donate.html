Tumaini Bora Africa

Overview

Tumaini Bora Africa (TBA) is a Community Based Organization registered to operate in Narok County with a regional scope under the NGO Act of 1990 Non-governmental organization. TBA works as an implementing agency through partnership with other development organization in Kenya with all development donations received being taxed exempt.

Mission

Our mission is to educate the teenagers on academic, social, health and economic skills, life skills-based education, financial literacy, livelihoods training and health related activities.We also strive to get things like sanitary towels clothes and learning materials to the ones in need.By this we impact their livelihoods and help them achieve their dreams.
