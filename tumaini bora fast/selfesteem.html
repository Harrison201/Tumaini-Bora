What is low self Esteeem

Self esteem is the valuation one places on one's self in all areas of life.Self esteem is one's perception of him or herself.When our self-esteem is low, we percieve ourselves and our life in a more negative way. We also feel less able to take on the challenges that comes in life .

Low self esteem is characterized by lacking confidence feeling incompetent.Most of the cases one feels like a fraud in everything they have accomplished

One experiences withdawak sysmptons and does not want to interact with other people.Once is this state of mind one see's that everybody is against you.Any slight critic from anyone can trigger an emotional breakdown

Low self-esteem can be casued by a variety of factors.Some of the cases in most people in their childhood when they handle correction or critics in a wrong way.The following are most of the reasons why people have low self-esteem.:

How to keep away from Low self esteem chrisis

Low self-esteem should be avoided because of the damage it can cause in ones life.Low self esteem affects a person life on every sector from realationships with other to accademic performance and even productivity on the work place

  1. Take criticsm in a positve way and work on them to improve your self

  4. Keep away from friend or relatives who have a bad influence in your life eg by forcing you to do thing that even yourselfknow that they are harmful.Friends are supposed to build you and make you a better version of yourself

Overview

The Savude Foundation is a non-governmental,humanitarian, non-partisan and non-profit making organization. SaF aims at contributing to the general health, education and developmental needs of vulnerable individuals and communities in Kenya. Specifically, it focuses on empowering adolescent girls by building their academic, social, health and economic skills, life skills-based education, financial literacy, livelihoods training and health related activities.

Mission

Our mission is to educate the teenagers on academic, social, health and economic skills, life skills-based education, financial literacy, livelihoods training and health related activities.We also strive to get things like sanitary towels clothes and learning materials to the ones in need.By this we impact their livelihoods and help them achieve their dreams.
